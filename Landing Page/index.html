About

Research has shown that having the right mix of calm and commotion in an environment can boost creativity. Sometimes, we go to cafes to get work done or to study. Other times, we head to cafes to meet people or to brainstorm for ideas. The reason why lovelyCafe is so popular is because it is not just a place for coffee, but also plays the role of “Third Place” – a comfortable, sociable gathering spot away from home and work.It is the combination of the various factors – good coffee, nice music, comfortable setting, andcomfortable food

We are proud of our interiors.
